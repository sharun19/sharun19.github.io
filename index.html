Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I use the app?
    To use the AI app, simply launch the app and start talking to the AI. The AI will respond to your questions and provide information or assistance as needed. You can also customize your settings and preferences within the app.
  • What features are included in the app?
    The AI app includes a powerful AI engine that can answer questions and provide information on a wide range of topics. Additionally, the app includes a user-friendly interface, customizable settings and preferences, and regular updates with new features and improvements.
  • Is the app available in different languages?
    At the moment, the AI app is only available in English. However, we are working on adding support for other languages in future updates
